vastu shastra

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science that focuses on harmonizing the environment with the individual. Key aspects of Vastu Shastra include:

(1)Directions: Emphasizes the importance of cardinal directions (north, south, east, west) and their influence on various aspects of life.

(2) Five Elements: Considers the balance of the five elements (earth, water, fire, air, and space) within a space to create harmony and positive energy flow.

(3) Layout: Provides guidelines for the layout of rooms, placement of furniture, and organization of spaces to optimize energy flow and enhance functionality.

(4) Entrance: Stresses the significance of the main entrance in welcoming positive energy and opportunities into the space.

(5) Materials: Recommends the use of specific materials and colors to enhance the energy of a space and create a conducive environment.

(6) Remedies: Offers remedies and corrections to mitigate any Vastu doshas (flaws) in existing structures, such as the use of mirrors, plants, or certain rituals.
